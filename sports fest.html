Sport Fest is a sports fair, but also much more than that. It is an event that aims to combine the passion for sports with healthy lifestyle.

Rules:
• Multiple teams from the same college are allowed.
• All the participants must belong to the same institute.
• Teams can comprise of members either both from same year or from different years.
• All other details shall be shared with the teams registering in due to this event of time.
• All decisions in matters of eligibility, authenticity and final judgments will be with organizing committee of IIITDM JABALPUR.
• The format and structure of events is subject to change before the actual beginning of the event.
