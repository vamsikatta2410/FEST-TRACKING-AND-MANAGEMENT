Music Club brings to you a myriad of musical events at one place. With competitions ranging from Solo singing, to Music Walk and Aaveg-the rock band competition; Saaz- Music Club, IIITDM Jabalpur invites you to compete for prizes.

Rules:
• Each team can have maximum 7 participants and minimum 4 participants.
• All the participants must belong to the IIITDM JABALPUR
• A student cannot be part of more than one team.
• Teams can comprise of members either both from same year or from different years.
• All decisions in matters of eligibility, authenticity and final judgments will be with organizing By management IIITDM JABALPUR.
• The format and structure of events is subject to change before the actual beginning of the event.
